Our 5 Step Talent Hiring Process

Hiring specialized teams of web developers and designers couldn’t get simpler than this. Keeping the best level of client experience in our mind, we’ve devised a five step hiring method for IT Staffing.

Step 1: Analysis

We study your requirements and expectations, ask questions and get clarity, prepare notes and then source you the top-class resources suitable for your existing requirement.

Step 2: Consultation

We hunt for the suitable candidates and the profiles of shortlisted candidates are shared, so that you can choose the ideal person for your requirement.

Step 3: Placement

The employed resources (designer/ developer) are allocated work and official procedure according to the selected package.

Step 4: Collaboration

The committed employee initiates work on the project once the first payment is received.

Step 5: Manage

The project timelines can be managed by assigning work directly to the selected resource.
